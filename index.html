About Us

Dev Digital is a Development group for MSFS2020. We are currently working on a Boeing 757, with more to come. We started this group in August of 2021 but started working on this project around January of 2021. We are hoping to release a 757-300, 757-200 and -200F, with the 757-300 being the first to release. If you want to keep up to date with our progress make sure to join our discord (Click the discord icon bellow). Dev digital currently consists of 1 developer and around 10 staff.
